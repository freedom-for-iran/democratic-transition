Subject:
Iran: Urgent Appeal Regarding Democratic Transition and Human Rights
Email Body:
Dear Distinguished Representatives of the Federal Foreign Office and German Political Leaders, In recent weeks, global attention has once again focused on Iran and the aspirations of its people. In January 2026, millions of citizens took to the streets to demand freedom, popular sovereignty, and a better future for their country. Despite severe repression and the tragic loss of thousands of lives, these courageous men and women continue to stand resolutely for liberation and an end to the authoritarian rule of the Islamic Republic. Their message is clear: Iranians seek a democratic system grounded in liberty, the rule of law, and respect for fundamental human rights. Across Iran and within the global Iranian diaspora, there is growing consensus that the country's future must be determined by its people, anchored in human rights, democratic governance, and the preservation of Iran's territorial integrity. Following the escalation of conflict on 28 February, urgent concerns have arisen regarding civilian safety, the nation's political future, territorial unity, and regional stability. In this context, the name of Reza Pahlavi has gained prominence across Iran and at protest gatherings worldwide. In recent years, he has emerged as the most widely recognized figure within the Iranian opposition, viewed by millions as a potential unifying leader during a transitional period toward democracy. Many Iranians, both inside the country and across the diaspora, look to the German government and international institutions to: 1. Respect the Iranian people's right to determine their political future. 2. Recognize Reza Pahlavi as a potential transitional leader guiding Iran toward democratic governance. 3. Protect civilians and prevent further harm amid ongoing conflict. 4. Support a free, internationally supervised referendum following the Islamic Republic's end, enabling Iranians to choose their system of government. 5. Uphold human rights and fundamental freedoms. 6. Preserve Iran's national unity and territorial integrity, discouraging separatism that could destabilize the country and region. 7. Ensure conditions for Iranians to freely and peacefully decide their nation's future. Ultimately, Iran's future must be determined by its people through free, democratic, and transparent processes. The international community has a vital role in ensuring that the voices of millions of Iranians seeking freedom, dignity, and democracy are heard and respected. Respectfully, [Your Name & Surname – City]
